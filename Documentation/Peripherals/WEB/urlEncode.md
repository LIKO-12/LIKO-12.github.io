# WEB.urlEncode
---

Encode a url to send it.

---

* **Available since:** _WEB:_ v1.0.0, _LIKO-12_: v0.6.0
* **Last updated in:** _WEB:_ v1.0.0, _LIKO-12_: v0.6.0

---

```lua
local url = WEB.urlEncode(url)
```

---
### Arguments
---

* **url (string):** URL to encode.


---
### Returns
---

* **url (string):** The encoded URL.
